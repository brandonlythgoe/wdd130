Ryan Lythgoe - Founder & Head Counselor

Ryan has had his fair share of battles throughout his life. He has struggled with mental illness, trauma, and addiction. These experiences have helped him to become the person he is today. Instead of allowing these struggles to discourage him and weigh him down, He has used them to learn, grow, and help others. His biggest strength is the empathy he has gained because of the trials he has faced. This empathy, combined with his knowledge make him one of the most sought after counselors in the U.S. Ryan has a Degree in Social Work, and a Ph.D in Pyschology.

Our Mission

Warriors of light was founded to help those fighting mental health illneses and addiction. Our goal is to provide the resources necessg.ary to help anyone seeking help. We know the difficulty of battling with mental illness and addiction and know how to help. Our first priority is helping you understand who you are, and help you understand your identity. Our focus on the individual allows you to build self-awareness, confidence, and find inner peace. We believe everyone deserves to feel loved, especially by themselves, and will strive every day to help you feel that love. We look forward to having you join the Warriors Of Light family.
