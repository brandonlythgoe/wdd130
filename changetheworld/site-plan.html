Overview

Purpose

Create a website for my brothers future rehab center. Provide the details of what the rehab center will do and use it as a template for what we want the actual website to be one day. My brother Ryan and I have the goal of making a real difference for those struggling with mental health issues and addictions. With His expertise in the medical field, and my knowledge in the tech industry we hope to help everyone increase their exposure and knowledge of their mental health and those who are struggling with addictions. Our goal is to provide care and assistance to anyone in need and to help the world be more aware of those who may be struggling around them.

Audience

Anyone struggling with mental illness or addiction as well as their close friends and family members.

Content

Home Page

The Home Page will show a general descrpition of what warriors of light rehab center offers, as well as our mission statement. I hope this site will one day help those in need be able to have access to the resources that they need in order to get help. This site will serve as the template if you will for the real warriors of light site that me and my brother eventually launch and will help me and my brother have an idea of what we want to accomplish.

Images for the Home page

Mental Health resources

The Mental Health resources page will provide helpful articles, videos, and advice for dealing with mental health struggles and addictions. This will be not only for future patients, but for close friends and family and anyone who is wanting to learn more about mental health.
